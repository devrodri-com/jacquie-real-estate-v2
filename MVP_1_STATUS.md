## 4. Strategic Decisions

- The site is trust-led, not inventory-first.
- Property Management remains the primary business axis.
- Listings support credibility and commercial readiness without turning the site into a portal.
- Preconstruction is presented as a curated catalog, not as an aggressive investment marketplace.
- Sensitive or unverified claims are not positioned as promises.
- ROI, guaranteed returns, EB-5, leaseback, STR, tax/legal claims, urgency language, and similar claims are avoided in public-facing copy.
- FR-CA is treated as a localized web experience.
- The site does not promise direct human service in French.
- WhatsApp remains the primary conversion path.

## 5. Known Risks / Future Polish

- Baccarat and Cipriani may still need editorial cleanup for duplicated highlights or residence details.
- Some project descriptions may still contain occasional sales-like phrasing that should be refined in a future editorial pass.
- Delivery formats may be inconsistent across projects and should be normalized.
- Reservation amounts and payment plan details should be verified against current official material.
- Payment plans should remain subject to confirmation and should not be treated as final commercial terms.
- MLS / SEFMLS watermarks on listing photos are accepted for MVP / preview, but clean versions should be requested if Jacquie or Miami Life Realty can provide them.
- Final manual mobile QA should still be done before sharing broadly.
- MLS / IDX compliance should be formally confirmed if the site is used as a public production property marketing channel.
- Final custom domain is still pending.
